We treat data governance not as a documentation exercise but as an engineering discipline. Compliance assertions, schema contracts, and lineage tracking are built directly into pipeline execution — not layered on top after the fact.

### What We Deliver

- **Automated Data Lineage** — Column-level lineage tracking that traces every metric from its raw source through every transformation to its final reporting surface.
- **Schema Contracts** — Enforced interface definitions between data producers and consumers, preventing silent breaking changes from propagating downstream.
- **Pipeline Quality Gates** — Unit-test assertions embedded directly into transformation runs. Pipelines that fail data quality checks do not proceed to production.
- **Data Catalog Integration** — Structured metadata emission to catalog tools (dbt docs, DataHub, Atlan) so your teams can self-serve documentation without relying on tribal knowledge.

### Our Approach

Governance failures are engineering failures. We instrument pipelines to surface anomalies immediately, not in retrospective audits. Our governance frameworks are owned by your team from delivery day one.

### Typical Engagement Outcomes

Organizations we work with achieve full column-level lineage across all production tables, elimination of undocumented data assets, and passing compliance audits without manual evidence collection.
