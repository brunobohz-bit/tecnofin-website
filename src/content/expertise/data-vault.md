For organizations operating in high-velocity, high-change environments, we design agile historical structures using Data Vault 2.0 methodology. By isolating hubs, links, and satellites, we guarantee that analytical schemas can scale horizontally and absorb raw operational migrations without structural breakage.

### What We Deliver

- **Hub, Link & Satellite Design** — Precise identification and modeling of business keys, relationships, and descriptive attributes with full historical tracking.
- **Raw Vault Construction** — Automated ingestion layers that load data exactly as received from source systems, preserving full audit trails.
- **Business Vault Layer** — Calculated satellites and bridge tables that enforce business rules downstream of the raw layer, keeping source data uncorrupted.
- **Point-in-Time & Bridge Tables** — Optimized query structures that restore snapshot views of historical data without full scans of satellite history.

### Our Approach

Data Vault is not a silver bullet — it is an architectural discipline. We apply it selectively, where data volatility, regulatory requirements, or multi-source integration justify the overhead. We do not over-engineer simple analytical domains.

### Typical Engagement Outcomes

Organizations we work with gain complete historical traceability from day one of ingestion, schema flexibility that accommodates new source systems without redesign, and full lineage from source to reporting layer.
